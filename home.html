There's more to music than notes

So we're going to change that. Introducing Brio: a modern open-source music search engine which offers you everything there is to know about a particular song. We're constantly adding new songs in collaboration with our users, and we're constantly improving. By using our algorithm you'll find out more than just a few minor details. Brio offers you everything at your feet.


How it works

We currently maintain a very large music database to which we add entries manually. The community has a very large contribution to this and we encourage everyone to add entries where they are due. We're still expanding with dozens of new songs added to the database every day.
